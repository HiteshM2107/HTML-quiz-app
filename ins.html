Instructions for the exam:

  • The exams consists of 10 questions
  • each question has four options out of which only ONE is correct.
  • For each correct answer, you score +4 marks.
  • For each wrong answer, you score -1 mark.
  • After completing the exam, click on Submit button and you will be directed to the results page.
  • The time duration is 5 mins. That is you get 30 seconds for each question. After 5 mins, the exam will automatically get terminated and you will be directed to the results page.
  • Make sure your other tabs are closed for a better Internet connectivity.
